Distribution Strategy

Door-to-Door

Visit neighbors personally. This is the most effective way to ensure people receive and read the information.

  • Best times: Evenings and weekends
  • Be prepared to answer questions
  • Leave door hangers if no one's home

Community Events

Set up information tables at local farmers markets, community events, and neighborhood gatherings.

  • Bring clipboards for petition signatures
  • Have multiple flyer types available
  • Engage people in conversation

Strategic Locations

Post flyers (with permission) at community centers, libraries, coffee shops, and other high-traffic areas.

  • Always ask permission first
  • Use community bulletin boards
  • Check and refresh regularly

Printing & Production Tips

Home Printing

  • Use high-quality paper (at least 24lb)
  • Print in color for maximum impact
  • Check ink levels before large batches
  • Consider cardstock for durability

Professional Printing

  • Local print shops often offer bulk discounts
  • Office supply stores have self-service options
  • Online printing for large quantities
  • Weather-resistant options for outdoor use

Cost-Effective Options

  • Black and white for budget-friendly printing
  • Coordinate bulk orders with neighbors
  • Use lighter paper weights for door hangers
  • Double-sided printing saves paper
