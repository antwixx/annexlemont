Sample Contact Message

Subject: Opposition to Commercial Rezoning on Lemont Road

Dear [Official Name],

I am writing as a concerned resident to express my strong opposition to the proposed commercial rezoning of residential properties on Lemont Road for the development of a truck stop facility.

This development would negatively impact our neighborhood by:

  • Increasing traffic congestion and safety concerns
  • Reducing property values in the residential area
  • Creating noise and air pollution
  • Disrupting the residential character of our community

I urge you to vote against any commercial rezoning applications for these properties and to protect the residential nature of our neighborhood.

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Address]
